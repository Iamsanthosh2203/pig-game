Rules

Here are the basic rules of the Pig Game:

  1. Each turn, a player repeatedly rolls a six-sided die until either a 1 is rolled or the player decides to "hold".
  2. If the player rolls a 1, they score nothing for that turn, and it becomes the next player's turn.
  3. If the player rolls any other number (2-6), the number is added to their turn total and they can choose to roll again or hold.
  4. If the player chooses to "hold", their turn total is added to their overall score, and it becomes the next player's turn.
  5. The game continues until one player reaches the target score (e.g., 100 points) and is declared the winner.
